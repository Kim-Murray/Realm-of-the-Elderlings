The Realm of the Elderlings, a 16 book series by Robin Hobb. Begin with The Farseer Trilogy (pictured above) and meet Fitz, a 6 year old boy, cast out by his maternal grandfather and sent to live at the palace in Buckkeep. Simultaneously a member of the royal family and an outcast, Fitz is trained to become a royal assassin to King Shrewd. As he grows to accept his fate and put his life on the line for the good of the Realm he begins to discover he may have other abilities that could get him into a lot of trouble should others discover them.
